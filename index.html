Portugal

The forest is not very dense and the main cause of forest destruction is fires. They can arise through human intervention. Without intention or with a purpose. They may also arise due to weather conditions such as high temperatures or thunderstorms. Portugal is heavily affected by fires, mainly in summer.

Russia

Russia has the largest area of forests of any state on Earth, with around 12 million km2 of boreal forest, larger than the Amazon rainforest. Russia's forests contain 55% of the world's conifers and represent 11% of biomass on Earth.

Brazil

Brazil once had the highest deforestation rate in the world. The removal of forest to make way for cattle ranching was the leading cause of deforestation in the Brazilian Amazon from the mid- 1960s. It was also seen by developers as an economic investment during periods of high inflation.

Indonesia

Private corporations, motivated by economic profits from local and regional market, are guilty for deforestation. These companies often do not comply with the basic legal regulations by inappropriately employing cost effective yet environmentally inefficient deforestation methods.
